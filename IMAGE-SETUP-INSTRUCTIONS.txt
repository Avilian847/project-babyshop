STEP 1: Create the folders
---------------------------
1. In the same folder as your HTML file, create a folder called "images"
2. Inside "images", create a folder called "gallery"


STEP 2: Add your logo
----------------------
1. Put your logo file in the "images" folder
2. Rename it to "logo.png" (or keep the name and update the CSS)
3. If your logo has a different name, update line 145 & 156 in Avilian.css:
   
   Change: background-image: url('images/logo.png');
   To: background-image: url('images/YOUR-LOGO-NAME.png');


STEP 3: Add your gallery images
--------------------------------
1. Put your 9 product images in the "images/gallery" folder
2. Rename them: image1.jpg, image2.jpg, image3.jpg, ... image9.jpg
   
   OR
   
   Keep your original filenames and update the HTML (lines 139-173):
   
   Change: <img src="images/gallery/image1.jpg" alt="Baby product 1">
   To: <img src="images/gallery/YOUR-IMAGE-NAME.jpg" alt="Baby product 1">
